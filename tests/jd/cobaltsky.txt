Who we are & why should you join

Cobalt Sky provide award-winning IT services to the market research industry, combining customer-focused service with technical expertise. No technical or computing experience is necessary - we are looking for a service-minded graduate to join our team and we provide all training in-house. So long as you enjoy problem-solving and have strong attention to detail combined with good communication skills, we can teach you the rest.

We have a great reputation in our industry for providing our clients with work of the highest quality & provide excellent training and support to help develop our staff both professionally and personally. We have a vibrant, energetic and dedicated team, the majority of whom joined our company as graduates and have grown into senior consultants with us.

We are proud signatories of the Market Research Society’s Inclusion Pledge and are committed to creating an inclusive workplace environment where the only barriers to progress are personal choice and professional competence. Our teams are made up of people with different strengths, experiences, lifestyles and backgrounds and we believe that diversity is a key to our success.

Your starting salary will be £27,000 for the first 3 months. Once you pass the probation period and are on a permanent contract the position is then paid £30,000.

Job Description

Cobalt Sky program and host online surveys and transform the data collected from them into a variety of reports to our clients. You will be taught to use a range of software and programming languages to ensure you’re well equipped for the job.

This is a client-facing role so you’ll need good written and verbal communication - once you’ve completed your initial training you will be working directly with our clients running your own projects.

Whilst the role is technical in nature and we teach and use lots of different software and programming languages, this role is suitable for someone looking for a career as a pure computer programmer.

Key Skills

· Attention to detail

· Written communication

· Verbal communication

· Desire to deliver a high standard of work

· Ability to work well in a team

· Good time management and ability to work well under pressure

· Proactive and self-motivated

Requirements

· You must have a degree (any discipline) and ideally you will have graduated within the last 2 years.

· Once your initial training is successfully completed, we offer flexible working with a mix of home and office

· You must have the permanent right to live and work in the UK.
