Job Description


We are hiring individuals who take a structured, agentic approach to solving real-world problems using modern, AI-enabled tools.

This role is suited to people who are naturally curious, analytical, and motivated by understanding how things work. You will focus on breaking down complex problems, designing structured solutions, and iterating towards effective outcomes, working with both foundational technical concepts and AI-assisted workflows.

This is not a traditional code-first development role. While coding and debugging may form part of your growth, the primary focus is on problem definition, logical decomposition, and solution design.

You will work closely with experienced architects and business analysts to translate business challenges into clear, structured solutions, including workflows, data flows, decision models, and specifications.

You will operate within a modern, well-architected enterprise environment, leveraging AI-assisted platforms, low-code tooling, and data-centric systems such as Microsoft Copilot Studio / Power Platform / Power Automate and related ecosystems.

Success in this role requires a disciplined, methodical mindset, the confidence to challenge assumptions, and a genuine interest in solving problems that have practical impact.

Key Responsibilities

    Analyse business problems and translate them into structured solution approaches
    Create artefacts such as workflows, data flows, diagrams, decision trees, and pseudo code
    Use AI-assisted tools to explore, validate, and refine solutions in a controlled and structured way
    Critically assess AI-generated outputs, identifying gaps, inconsistencies, and risks
    Apply logical, heuristic, and lateral thinking to iterate and improve solutions
    Work with core system concepts such as data structures, databases, caching, lookups, indexing, and integrations
    Communicate clearly with stakeholders to ensure shared understanding of problems and solutions
    Contribute to solution refinement, testing, debugging, and continuous improvement
    Operate effectively within structured delivery practices and quality standards

Role Expectations

This role is best suited to individuals who:

    Prefer structured thinking over trial-and-error approaches
    Are comfortable working through ambiguity by applying logic and method
    Are motivated by continuous learning and improvement
    Take ownership of problems and see them through to resolution
    Balance discipline with adaptability when exploring solutions

Future Potential

This role is designed for individuals who may go on to become solution designers, technical leads, or architects over time.

We do not expect deep technical expertise from day one. Instead, we value clarity of thought, structured problem solving, and the willingness to learn.

You will be supported by experienced practitioners while developing your ability to:

    understand complex systems
    model processes and data flows
    apply proven problem-solving approaches
    work effectively with AI as part of solution delivery


Qualifications


Essential

    Background or strong interest in a structured or analytical discipline, such as science, engineering, mathematics, software, business systems, or similar
    Minimum A-Level or equivalent in an analytical subject; degree-level education is advantageous but not required
    Strong logical reasoning and problem decomposition skills
    Ability to express ideas clearly using diagrams, workflows, structured notes, or pseudo code
    Confidence communicating with both technical and non-technical stakeholders
    Curiosity about how systems, data, and processes work
    Comfortable using AI tools, with the ability to question and validate their outputs
    Pragmatic mindset with a willingness to test ideas, learn quickly, and improve

You are not expected to have all of the above from day one. We are particularly interested in how you think and approach problems.

Desirable

Exposure to or awareness of the following concepts is beneficial, but not required.

    Awareness of modern solution environments, including:
        Low-code / no-code platforms and AI-assisted development tools
        Integration patterns using APIs, connectors, and event-driven architectures
    Understanding of core software and system design concepts such as:
        Microservices and distributed systems
        Data flows and messaging patterns
        Contract-based design and loosely coupled architectures
    Awareness of data-focused principles, including:
        Data modelling and data lifecycle
        Data governance and quality considerations
        Challenges around single source of truth and data consistency
    Exposure to data platforms or analytics tools, such as data warehouses, reporting platforms, or semantic layers
    Familiarity with modern enterprise platforms, such as Microsoft Copilot Studio / Power Platform / Power Automate or similar ecosystems
    Understanding of structured delivery practices, such as SDLC, versioning, testing, and quality gates
