We are looking for an experienced Agentic AI Instructor to teach a 6- or 12-week, full-time immersive bootcamp focused on building production-ready agentic AI systems. The program takes learners from LLM fundamentals through RAG, single- and multi-agent systems, AgentOps, and deployment.

This role is open to both in-person and remote instructors.

For in-person delivery, instructors must be available to travel to Saudi Arabia for an initial 3-month engagement, with the possibility of extension to 9–12 months or longer.

What You Will Teach

You will guide learners through an end-to-end, capstone-driven curriculum covering:

    Python fundamentals and Generative AI basics (pre-work alignment)
    LLM fundamentals, prompting, evaluation, and safety
    AI-assisted development and “vibe coding”
    Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with vector databases
    Single-agent systems with reasoning loops and tool use
    Multi-agent systems with memory, planning, and coordination
    Agentic platforms (e.g., LangGraph, AutoGen concepts)
    Agent reliability, safety, guardrails, and AgentOps
    Observability, cost control, and performance optimization
    Deployment patterns and production-ready agentic architectures
    Capstone mentoring and technical demo preparation
